    About us

    We are a group of young students from Adlerka Secondary Vocational School, aged 15 to 17 years old, participating in this year’s CanSat competition. Despite the fact that we are students with different interests and goals in life, we all came together to take on this challenge in pursuit of knowledge and new experiences. All of us are very excited to embark on our first-ever space-oriented project, initiated by the European Space Agency.
