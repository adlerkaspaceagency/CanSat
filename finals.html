    We’re Going to the Finals!

    After an incredible amount of hard work and preparation, we’re proud to announce that our team has advanced to the finals!

    It’s been an amazing journey so far. Following the successful presentation of our project at the Slovak University of Technology in Bratislava, we’ve been selected as one of the few teams to move forward to the regional finals. We’re truly grateful to the judges and the entire STU team involved in the evaluation and voting process. A special thank-you goes out to the organizing committee for the warm welcome—and, of course, the excellent snacks!

    As finalists, we’re fully committed to making the most of this opportunity. The final stretch will require continued testing, fine-tuning, and perseverance—but we’re ready for the challenge.

    Your support has brought us this far, and we’ll need it more than ever as we enter this exciting next phase. Stay with us—there’s still a lot to come, and we can’t wait to share it with you.

    See you at the finals!
