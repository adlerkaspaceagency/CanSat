    Finals Recap: An Unforgettable Ride

    Our CanSat journey has officially reached its final chapter — at least for this season — and what a ride it’s been. While we didn’t make it into the top three, we’re walking away with something far more important: growth, experience, and memories that will last a lifetime.

    Getting to the finals wasn’t a walk in the park. It meant long nights, technical setbacks, and challenges that constantly pushed us to adapt and improve. But every obstacle came with a lesson, and by the time we arrived at the finals, we were proud of how much we’d grown – not just as a team, but as individuals.

    The finals were held at the Malé Bielice airport in central Slovakia, where 11 teams from across the country gathered to launch their CanSats using a drone provided by organizers. One by one, each project took off to the skies, including ours. The flight went smoothly, and so did our presentation. We gave it everything we had.

    Your support has brought us this far, and we’ll need it more than ever as we enter this exciting next phase. Stay with us—there’s still a lot to come, and we can’t wait to share it with you.

    This may be the end of one chapter, but it’s far from the end of the story.

    We’ll be back – better, smarter, and more determined than ever.
