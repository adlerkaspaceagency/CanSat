    First Steps in Making a CanSat

    Deciding the Secondary Mission

    When it came to choosing our secondary mission for the CanSat project, the decision was far from easy. We came up with quite a few ideas, each of which had its own potential. Our suggestions ranged from scanning movement of people or animals on the ground to measuring UV radiation, all of which would have been meaningful, but we have chosen Air Pollution Monitoring due to its scope in today’s world.

    Building the Website

    Building our website is an exciting part of our journey. We want to create a platform where everyone can follow our progress, learn about our mission, and explore the process of creating a mini satellite. Our goal is to make it engaging and accessible for students, teachers, and anyone passionate about space and the environment. For that, we will be using features like updates and photos. Stay tuned as we share our milestones, challenges, and successes along the way!

    Gathering Sponsors

    Securing sponsorship is a vital part of our journey. We reach out to organizations that share our passion for innovation, education, and environmental awareness. Each sponsor’s support brings us closer to achieving our goals, turning ideas into action and inspiring future change.
