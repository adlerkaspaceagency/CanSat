    Our mission

    As our objective, we have chosen the vast field of “Air pollution”. Measuring elements, such as carbon dioxide and sulfur dioxide may not seem like a very important task to do, but these elements greatly affect our environment and us. Our Earth’s temperature has been rising over the years mainly because of carbon dioxide, which is the leading gas in greenhouse gases, and sulfur dioxide, produced primarily by fossil fuels, has damaged trees and plants all over the world. Due to these facts, we are going to measure these elements and others that pollute our air. With the data collected, we want to determine the extent of the problem more accurately.

    Influence

    With this project we want to collect data with higher precision in specific locations to gain a further understanding of this problem. With already analyzed and organized data, we can then send it to responsible authorities, who could possibly use this data to adjust their readings in certain areas or update them. With a higher understanding of this issue, more suitable measures can be taken, which would positively influence us, our nature and the environment we live in.
