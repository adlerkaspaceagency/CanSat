    We've Made It to the Semifinals!

    We’re excited to share some great news — our team has officially advanced to the semifinals!

    After weeks of intense work, long evenings, and countless revisions, we submitted our Critical Design Review (CDR) report — and it was strong enough to carry us into the next stage of the competition. None of this would have been possible without the dedication, creativity, and collaboration of our amazing team.

    This Friday, we’ll be presenting our progress at the Slovak University of Technology (STU) in Bratislava. Many parts of our project are still evolving, going through testing and improvements, but we’re proud of everything we’ve accomplished over the past few months. It’s been a journey full of learning, growth, and discoveries — and we’re only just getting started.

    Stay tuned as we move into the next phase! We’ll keep you updated along the way, and your support means a great deal to us. There are exciting things ahead — and we can’t wait to share them with you.
